How to Order?

Clients

  1. 01

    Application Receipt

    Contact us in any convenient way — we'll answer all your questions and listen to your wishes.

  2. 02

    Signing the Contract

    We document all your criteria and your maximum budget, then you make a deposit which is fully refundable up until the car is purchased.

  3. 03

    Selecting & Purchasing the Car

    Your personal manager selects options daily based on your criteria, shares detailed information on each lot, and agrees the bid with you. If the car is won, the auction house issues an invoice which you pay yourself at a bank in your own city.

  4. 04

    Delivery to the Port

    After payment, we deliver the purchased car from the auction to the port, where it awaits loading for shipment to Vladivostok.

  5. 05

    Customs Clearance

    We submit customs documents for the individual the car will be cleared under. Vehicles clear customs without the owner needing to be present.

  6. 06

    Vehicle Handover

    We hand the car to you in person in Vladivostok, or send it to your city by any convenient method (car carrier / rail).
